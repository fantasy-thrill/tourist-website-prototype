Plan your getaway to the island of Taniti!

Take a chance to explore everything our island has to offer!

Taniti is a charming tropical island in the Pacific that offers visitors a perfect balance of relaxation and adventure. Tourists can enjoy a variety of dining options, from fresh local seafood and rice dishes to American-style meals and Pan-Asian cuisine. Entertainment is plentiful, with activities ranging from snorkeling, zip-lining, and chartered fishing tours to exploring art galleries, pubs, and even a new dance club. For those seeking a more laid-back experience, Taniti boasts beautiful white-sand beaches around Yellow Leaf Bay, rainforest hikes, and breathtaking views of its active volcano. With a wide range of accommodations — from family-run hotels to a luxury four-star resort — Taniti welcomes travelers looking for both comfort and cultural charm.
